# 🌱 First Contribution Guide

Your first open-source contribution can be as small as fixing a typo. Small is how everyone starts.

## Practice safely

- [First Contributions](https://github.com/firstcontributions/first-contributions) — a practice repo built for your literal first pull request.
- [First Timers Only](https://www.firsttimersonly.com/) — the philosophy and starting point for first-time contributors.

## The full walkthrough

- [How to Contribute to Open Source](https://opensource.guide/how-to-contribute/) — GitHub's canonical guide.
- [SoterCare First Contribution Guide](https://github.com/SoterCare/community/blob/main/guides/first-contribution.md) — our step-by-step, mentor-supported onboarding.

## Find a first issue

- [Good First Issue](https://goodfirstissue.dev/) — curated beginner-friendly issues.
- [CodeTriage](https://www.codetriage.com/) — get assigned open issues from projects you care about.

## Programs that welcome beginners

- [GirlScript Summer of Code](https://gssoc.girlscript.tech/) — beginner-friendly; SoterCare is an Ambassador & Contributor.
- [Hacktoberfest](https://hacktoberfest.com/) — the annual celebration of open source every October.
