# 🌍 Open Source Programs

Structured programs that pay you, mentor you, or recognize you for contributing to open source.

## Global programs

- [Google Summer of Code](https://summerofcode.withgoogle.com/) — paid open-source contributions with mentoring, for all skill levels.
- [Outreachy](https://www.outreachy.org/) — paid, remote internships for people underrepresented in tech.
- [MLH Fellowship](https://fellowship.mlh.io/) — a remote, internship-alternative building open-source software.
- [LFX Mentorship (Linux Foundation)](https://lfx.linuxfoundation.org/tools/mentorship/) — paid mentorships across major open-source projects.
- [Hacktoberfest](https://hacktoberfest.com/) — the annual celebration of open source every October.

## Student-run programs

- [GirlScript Summer of Code](https://gssoc.girlscript.tech/) — beginner-friendly; SoterCare is an Ambassador & Contributor.
- [Nexus Spring of Code](https://github.com/SoterCare/community/blob/main/partners/nsoc.md) — an open-source program SoterCare partners with.

## Developer communities

- [GitHub Community](https://github.com/orgs/community/discussions) — discussions and support from GitHub's community.
- [dev.to](https://dev.to/) — a friendly community of developers writing and sharing.
- [SoterCare Community](https://github.com/SoterCare/community) — join us: events, mentorship, and first contributions.
