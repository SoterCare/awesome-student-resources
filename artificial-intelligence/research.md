# 🔬 AI Research

Read papers, follow the frontier, and learn to do research.

## Find papers

- [arXiv (cs.AI / cs.LG)](https://arxiv.org/list/cs.LG/recent) — where nearly every AI paper is published first, free.
- [Papers with Code](https://paperswithcode.com/) — papers paired with their code and benchmark results.
- [Hugging Face Papers](https://huggingface.co/papers) — trending papers, curated daily.
- [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/) — AI-powered academic search.

## Understand papers

- [Distill.pub](https://distill.pub/) — beautifully clear explanations of ML research concepts.
- [The Illustrated Transformer](https://jalammar.github.io/illustrated-transformer/) — the classic visual explanation of transformers.
- [Yannic Kilcher (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/@YannicKilcher) — accessible paper walkthroughs.

## Learn to research

- [How to Read a Paper (S. Keshav)](https://web.stanford.edu/class/ee384m/Handouts/HowtoReadPaper.pdf) — the classic three-pass method, free PDF.
- [Distill — Research Debt](https://distill.pub/2017/research-debt/) — how to think about and communicate research clearly.
