# 💼 Internship Resources

Where to find internships — including paid, remote, and open-source ones.

## Listings

- [Summer 2026 Internships (SimplifyJobs)](https://github.com/SimplifyJobs/Summer2026-Internships) — a huge, community-maintained list, updated daily.
- [levels.fyi Internships](https://www.levels.fyi/internships/) — internship listings with pay transparency.
- [LinkedIn — Internship Jobs](https://www.linkedin.com/jobs/internship-jobs/) — filter for student and internship roles.

## Paid open-source internships

- [Google Summer of Code](https://summerofcode.withgoogle.com/) — paid open-source contributions with mentoring.
- [Outreachy](https://www.outreachy.org/) — paid, remote internships for people underrepresented in tech.
- [Summer of Bitcoin](https://www.summerofbitcoin.org/) — a global internship in Bitcoin open source.

## Student programs

- [GitHub Campus Experts](https://education.github.com/experts) — become a student community leader (a great resume boost).
- [MLH Fellowship](https://fellowship.mlh.io/) — a remote internship alternative building open-source software.
