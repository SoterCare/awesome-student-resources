# 📄 Resume Resources

A great tech resume is short, specific, and results-focused. These help you build one.

## Guides

- [Tech Interview Handbook — Resume](https://www.techinterviewhandbook.org/resume/) — exactly what a strong tech resume needs, with examples.
- [Google — How to write a resume](https://grow.google/certificates/interview-warmup/) — practical guidance from Google's career resources.

## Templates

- [Jake's Resume (Overleaf)](https://www.overleaf.com/latex/templates/jakes-resume/syzfjbzwjncs) — the clean, ATS-friendly LaTeX template many engineers use.
- [Overleaf Resume Templates](https://www.overleaf.com/gallery/tagged/cv) — a gallery of free, professional templates.

## Get it reviewed

- [r/EngineeringResumes Wiki](https://www.reddit.com/r/EngineeringResumes/wiki/index/) — a detailed, community-maintained resume guide plus free reviews.

## Tips

- One page. Lead bullets with impact and numbers. Tailor keywords to the role. Link your GitHub and best projects.
