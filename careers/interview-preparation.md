# 🎯 Interview Preparation

Technical interviews are a learnable skill. Practice deliberately with these.

## Structured prep

- [Tech Interview Handbook](https://www.techinterviewhandbook.org/) — the best free, end-to-end interview guide.
- [NeetCode](https://neetcode.io/) — curated problem lists (incl. Blind 75) with clear video solutions.
- [Coding Interview University](https://github.com/jwasham/coding-interview-university) — a complete self-study plan, free.

## Practice problems

- [LeetCode](https://leetcode.com/) — the standard platform for coding-interview practice.
- [HackerRank](https://www.hackerrank.com/) — problems plus skill certifications.

## System design

- [System Design Primer](https://github.com/donnemartin/system-design-primer) — learn how large-scale systems are built, free.

## Behavioral

- [Google Interview Warmup](https://grow.google/certificates/interview-warmup/) — practice answering questions out loud with feedback.
