# 💰 Scholarships & Fellowships

Funding and fellowships that support students in tech.

## Fellowships

- [MLH Fellowship](https://fellowship.mlh.io/) — a paid, remote, internship-alternative building open-source software.
- [Outreachy](https://www.outreachy.org/) — paid, remote internships for people underrepresented in tech.
- [Google Summer of Code](https://summerofcode.withgoogle.com/) — a stipend for contributing to open source over the summer.

## Scholarships

- [Google Scholarships](https://buildyourfuture.withgoogle.com/scholarships) — a hub of Google's scholarship programs for students in tech.
- [GitHub Education](https://education.github.com/) — programs, packs, and opportunities for students and teachers.
- [Grace Hopper Celebration Scholarships](https://ghc.anitab.org/attend/scholarships/) — funding to attend the largest gathering of women in computing.

## Tip

Scholarship and fellowship deadlines come once a year — set reminders early, and apply even if you feel underqualified. Someone has to get them; it may as well be you.
